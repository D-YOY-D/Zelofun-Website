## 1. About the Service

Zelofun is a social overlay platform that enables users to:
- Post comments, reactions, images, videos, and voice notes on top of any website

The Service operates as a Chrome browser extension and Progressive Web App. It creates a transparent social layer above existing websites without modifying or interfering with those websites.
